ShipLedger Shipping Evidence Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Yohan Labs handles data for ShipLedger Shipping Evidence. Contact azij781123@gmail.com with privacy questions or deletion requests.

Shopify permissions

read_orders, read_fulfillments, read_merchant_managed_fulfillment_orders, and read_third_party_fulfillment_orders.

Data processed

Whitelisted operational order and fulfillment facts: order ID/name, dates, fulfillment status, shipping amounts, refund amounts, fulfillment-order statuses, tracking company, masked/hashed tracking information, tracking URL host, event timestamps, and merchant-entered incident notes.

Data stored

Shop settings, webhook event hashes, order snapshots, fulfillment-order snapshots, fulfillment snapshots, refund snapshots, detected exceptions, merchant reports, and evidence export metadata.

Data not stored

Customer names, emails, phone numbers, addresses, geolocation, zip codes, payment card data, and full tracking numbers.

Retention and uninstall

The app has a default retention setting of 180 days for operational evidence records. On uninstall, Shopify sessions are deleted and the shop is marked uninstalled. Contact support to request earlier deletion.

Shopify GDPR webhooks

Customer data request, customer redaction, shop redaction, and app uninstall webhooks are implemented and HMAC-verified. ShipLedger is designed to avoid storing customer identifying fields.

Subprocessors and infrastructure

The app runs on Cloudflare infrastructure. Data may be stored in Cloudflare D1 and, for RestoreDrill theme archives only, Cloudflare R2. Alert or email delivery providers are used only where the app feature requires them and the merchant configures that channel.
