ExactFind SKU & Part Search Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Yohan Labs handles data for ExactFind SKU & Part Search. Contact azij781123@gmail.com with privacy questions or deletion requests.

Shopify permissions

read_products and write_app_proxy.

Data processed

Product and variant data needed for exact lookup: product and variant IDs, handles, titles, SKU, barcode, vendor code, product image URL, availability, and published status. Storefront search requests are processed through a signed app proxy.

Data stored

A D1 index of variant identifiers, merchant-created aliases, sync state, and no-result queries with counts and last-seen timestamps.

Data not stored

Customer names, emails, phone numbers, addresses, orders, carts, payment data, and full storefront browsing histories.

Retention and uninstall

ExactFind keeps its identifier index and no-result query history while the app is installed so searches and alias reports keep working. On uninstall, Shopify sessions are deleted. Other app records may remain until manual deletion or routine maintenance is completed; contact support to request deletion.

Shopify GDPR webhooks

Customer data request, customer redaction, shop redaction, and app uninstall webhooks are implemented and HMAC-verified. Because ExactFind does not store customer personal data, customer webhooks are acknowledged without returning stored customer records.

Subprocessors and infrastructure

The app runs on Cloudflare infrastructure. Data may be stored in Cloudflare D1 and, for RestoreDrill theme archives only, Cloudflare R2. Alert or email delivery providers are used only where the app feature requires them and the merchant configures that channel.
