K-Trend Product Signal Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Yohan Labs handles data for K-Trend Product Signal. Contact azij781123@gmail.com with privacy questions or deletion requests.

Shopify permissions

read_products only.

Data processed

Product catalog fields used for matching and signal display: Shopify product and variant IDs, title, vendor, tags, SKU, barcode, price, currency, image URL, and update timestamps.

Data stored

A local product mirror, automatic and pinned product matches, product signals, digest event payloads, public market observations, crawl job metadata, actor run metadata, and app install records.

Data not stored

Customer data, orders, fulfillments, checkout data, payment data, and private customer behavior.

Retention and uninstall

Catalog mirrors, matches, signals, and digest records are kept while the app is installed because they power the dashboard and history. App uninstall marks the shop as uninstalled and deletes Shopify sessions. Contact support to request deletion of remaining app records.

Shopify GDPR webhooks

Mandatory customer data request, customer redaction, shop redaction, and app uninstall webhooks are implemented and HMAC-verified. K-Trend does not request or store customer personal data.

Subprocessors and infrastructure

The app runs on Cloudflare infrastructure. Data may be stored in Cloudflare D1 and, for RestoreDrill theme archives only, Cloudflare R2. Alert or email delivery providers are used only where the app feature requires them and the merchant configures that channel.
