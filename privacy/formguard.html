FormGuard Form Monitor Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Yohan Labs handles data for FormGuard Form Monitor. Contact azij781123@gmail.com with privacy questions or deletion requests.

Shopify permissions

write_app_proxy only. No customer, product, order, theme, or metaobject scopes are requested.

Data processed

Form submission values are processed transiently during the request so they can be forwarded to the merchant's configured email or Slack alert channel. Published form HTML may be scanned for likely accessibility issues.

Data stored

Shop settings, alert channel labels, form source metadata, submission event metadata, delivery attempt metadata, scan page URLs, scan run metadata, and scan issue records. Stored submission metadata can include field count, payload size, status, idempotency key, and an optional salted PII hash.

Data not stored

Raw form field values, message bodies, customer profiles, orders, payments, and Shopify customer records.

Retention and uninstall

Metadata retention defaults to 90 days. On uninstall, Shopify sessions are deleted and the shop is marked uninstalled. Contact support to request earlier deletion.

Shopify GDPR webhooks

Mandatory customer data request, customer redaction, shop redaction, and app uninstall webhooks are implemented and HMAC-verified. FormGuard does not request Shopify customer data and does not store raw submission values.

Subprocessors and infrastructure

The app runs on Cloudflare infrastructure. Data may be stored in Cloudflare D1 and, for RestoreDrill theme archives only, Cloudflare R2. Alert or email delivery providers are used only where the app feature requires them and the merchant configures that channel.
